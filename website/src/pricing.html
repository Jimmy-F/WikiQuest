Frequently asked questions

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, just email us within 7 days for a full refund.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) and PayPal through our secure Stripe payment processor.

How do streak freezes work?

Streak freezes automatically protect your daily streak if you miss a day. Pro users get 2 per week, Student users get 1 per week. They renew weekly.

What happens if I run out of hearts?

Hearts refill at 1 per hour automatically. Free users get 5 total. Or you can upgrade to Pro for unlimited hearts and never worry about it!

Can I use WikiQuest for my classroom?

Yes! We offer special Team/Classroom plans for educators. Contact us at education@wikiquest.io for bulk pricing and features.
